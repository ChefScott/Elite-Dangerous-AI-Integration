Data Collection Policy

Effective Date: 2024-06-14
Last Updated: 2024-06-14

Your Privacy Matters to Us

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Data Collection Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it when you use our application.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to improve our product and ensure its reliability. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Interaction amounts, time spent, and usage patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution.
  • Error Logs: Crash reports, stack traces, error messages.
  • Anonymized Identifiers: User IDs or session tokens (non-personal and non-identifiable).

2. Purpose of Collection

We collect and process this data solely for the following purposes:

  • Error Detection & Analysis: To identify bugs or performance issues.
  • Product Improvement: To understand how users interact with our app and make data-driven improvements.
  • Security & Stability: To protect our app from abuse and ensure reliable service.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis under the GDPR for collecting and processing this data is:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)) – We use the data to maintain and improve our services, provided such interests are not overridden by your data protection rights.
  • Where applicable, Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) – For any non-essential data (e.g., advanced analytics, model improvements), we will request your explicit opt-in consent.

4. Data Minimization and Retention

We minimize the data collected to only what is strictly necessary.

Personal identifiers are anonymized or pseudonymized whenever possible.

Data is retained for a maximum of 90 days unless longer retention is required for legal or technical reasons.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except:

  • With service providers (e.g., analytics or error monitoring tools) under strict data processing agreements.
  • When required by law or legal process.

All third parties are GDPR-compliant and bound by confidentiality obligations.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.

Withdraw your consent at any time (where consent was the legal basis).

Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: gdpr@covaslabs.com

7. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Collection Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via our application.

By continuing to use our application after changes are made, you agree to the updated policy.
